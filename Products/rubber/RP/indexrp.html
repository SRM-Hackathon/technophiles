Rubber Recycling

Rubber recycling is the process of recycling waste tires that are no longer suitable for use on vehicles due to wear or irreparable damage. These tires are a problematic source of waste, due to the large volume produced, the durability of the tires, and the components in the tire that are ecologically problematic.Because they are highly durable and non-biodegradable, they can consume valued space in landfills.
